Professional Animal Works (PAW)

Hetri Collar's livestream is not just a gimmick. Every frame, every dispense event and every help request gets recorded in our database so you know exactly what happened, when and why. You get a clear audit trail for each pet and instant owner satisfaction.

Behind the scenes our system analyzes collar telemetry such as activity bursts, rest cycles and weight to forecast staffing needs and optimize schedules before each day starts. No more last minute scramble or guesswork.

With PAW you deliver an unforgettable premium experience for pet parents while running your operations on data you can trust. Let us help you make your facility the one everyone talks about.

Booking & Billing

Seamless reservations, automated invoicing and payment capture with no manual data entry.

Staff Scheduling

Dynamic rosters driven by collar data and occupancy levels so you always have the right coverage.

Capacity Management

Live headcount dashboards and load balancing suggestions to maintain consistent service quality.

Telemetry Analytics

Rich insights from collar data to reveal trends, drive upsells and forecast staffing requirements.

Paperless Records

Digitally store vaccination records, consent forms and treat logs. All media is searchable and audit ready.

Alerts & Notifications

Battery, activity and assistance alerts flow directly into PAW so you never miss a critical event.

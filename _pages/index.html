Welcome to Hetri Inc.

At Hetri, we design and develop embedded systems that integrate seamlessly into everyday life. Our smart collar for doggy daycares provides real-time video access, giving pet owners peace of mind and creating transparency for care providers.

What We Do

We focus on creating real-time, user-friendly technology. Our solutions are built to solve specific problems in practical ways. The smart collar is just the beginning — a demonstration of our commitment to usable, elegant tech.

Our Vision

We aim to lead in developing intelligent systems that enhance how people live and interact with technology. Our roadmap is grounded in solving real needs with scalable, embedded solutions.
